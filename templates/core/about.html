About the College

Nasarawa State College of Health Sciences and Technology, Keffi is a government-owned institution dedicated to training competent health professionals who serve communities across Nigeria.

Our Story

From foundation to full college status

The College began in 1999 as a School, established by the first Executive Governor of Nasarawa State, Sen. Dr. Abdullahi Adamu. Its foundation was formally backed by legislation in 2002.

Following institutional reviews in 2015 and 2019, the School was elevated to a College. This transition was further strengthened by the current Governor, His Excellency Engr. A. A. Sule, who revised the enabling law to reflect the College’s expanded academic mandate.

Today, the College offers 11 National Diploma programmes, including 3 Higher National Diploma programmes. Graduates earn qualifications ranging from Professional Diplomas to Higher National Diplomas, preparing them for service in both primary and secondary healthcare facilities. All National Diploma programmes are fully accredited and recognized by relevant Professional Bodies and the National Board for Technical Education (NBTE).

Our Mission

We are committed to producing skilled and competent human resources for primary and secondary healthcare facilities through fully accredited programmes. Supported by state, national, and international organizations, the College responds to evolving health needs and challenges by harnessing the expertise of its academic staff to meet the demands of a dynamic and ever-changing world.

Application Procedure for Professional Diploma

  1. Visit the College website and navigate to the Admissions or Application section.
  2. Create an application profile using a valid email address and phone number.
  3. Complete the online application form with accurate personal and academic details.
  4. Select the desired programme of study.
  5. Upload required documents, including academic credentials and identification.
  6. Submit the application and print the acknowledgement slip.
  7. Applicants will be contacted for screening or further instructions where applicable.

Application Procedure – National Diploma (ND)

  1. Apply through the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) by selecting Nasarawa State College of Health Sciences and Technology, Keffi as your institution of choice.
  2. Upload your O’Level results (WAEC, NECO, or equivalent) to the JAMB portal.
  3. Ensure that your JAMB subject combination meets the requirements of the programme you intend to study. For most science-based programmes, this includes English Language, Mathematics, Biology, Physics, and Chemistry.
  4. After completing the JAMB application, await the College’s official invitation to participate in the aptitude test or screening exercise.
  5. Successful candidates will be guided through the remaining admission processes in line with College regulations.

Admission Requirements

Professional Diploma (PD)

Applicants must possess relevant secondary school qualifications or professional certificates as specified by the department.

National Diploma (ND)

Candidates must have a minimum of five (5) credit passes in relevant subjects, including English Language and Mathematics, obtained at not more than two sittings in SSCE, NECO, or equivalent examinations.

Higher National Diploma (HND)

Applicants must possess a National Diploma in a related discipline with a minimum of Lower Credit, along with evidence of one year industrial training where applicable.
