Admissions

Everything you need to know about applying to Nasarawa State College of Health Sciences and Technology, Keffi.

Application Procedure (National Diploma)

  1. Apply through the JAMB portal.
  2. Upload your O’Level results (WAEC / NECO or equivalent) on the JAMB platform.
  3. Ensure your subject combination matches programme requirements (English, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, Physics where applicable).
  4. Await invitation from the College for the aptitude test.
  5. Successful candidates proceed with further admission steps as directed by the College.

General Entry Requirements

  • Five (5) credit passes in relevant subjects including English and Mathematics
  • Credits must be obtained in not more than two sittings
  • Relevant science subjects depending on the programme

Frequently Asked Questions

Does meeting the minimum requirements guarantee admission?

No. Admission is competitive and subject to available slots and performance in the aptitude test.

Are all programmes accredited?

Yes. All National Diploma programmes are fully accredited by NBTE.

Can I apply without JAMB?

No. JAMB application is mandatory for National Diploma programmes.
